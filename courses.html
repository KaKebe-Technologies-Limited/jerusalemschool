Admission Guidelines

Certificate in: Nursing, Pharmacy, Midwifery, Medical theatre assistants

For those interested in joining our certificate course (Certificate in Nursing and Midwifery), you should be 18 years and above otherwise, it doesn’t matter. Forgery of result slips leads to automatic elimination from the course You should have scored at least passes in all science subjects and English at the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) level

Certificate in Medical records

Forgery of result slips leads to automatic elimination from the course You should have scored at least passes in Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and English English even though you have an f9 in physics at the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) level

Diploma in: Nursing extension, Midwifery extension

You should have completed your two years of practicing as per the practicing license or the enrollment form. You should have done a certificate course in your respective course you which to upgrade to.

Diploma in Pharmacy

For direct entrants, Applicants shall be required to have at least one principal pass in chemistry and a subsidiary in Biology and any related subject such as physics, mathematics at Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) For certificate holders, it should be a pharmacy certificate with outstanding performance. Anyone with a diploma or degree holder in any health-related course

Diploma in Clinical Medicine

For direct entrants, Applicants shall be required to have at least one principal pass in Biology and a subsidiary in Chemistry and any related subject such as Physics, Mathematics at Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) If you have a certificate in Nursing or Comprehensive nursing

Diploma in Radiography

For direct entrants, Applicants shall be required to have at least one principal pass in Physics and a subsidiary in Biology and any related subject such as Chemistry, Mathematics at Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) Certificate in any medical field.

Diploma in Health Service Management and Leadership

To do this course, the applicant must be working in the Health Sector with a minimum of a certificate in any health-related field e.g. clinical medicine, enrolled nursing, medical laboratory, etc.
