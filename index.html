Frequently Asked Questions


How do i know i qualify for Certificate Course?

Student must have passed science subjects with atleast a pass in English and Mathematics

How many intackes do you offer?

Nursing section intake is January and July and Allied section is in August..

How long is diploma course for someone who completed certificate in Nursing?

It is two years.

Is the institution registered?

Yes, the institution is registered with with Uganda Nurses and Midwives Council & Uganda Allied Health Professional Council?

What is the minimum requirement for a person to be admintted for a Diploma in Clinical Medicine & Community Health?

Atleast a principal pass in biology and subsidiary in Chemistry and principal pass in Chemistry and subsidiary in Biology

How do I qualify for diploma in Midwifery Extension?

Must have passed certificate in Midwifery and two years experience from the time you registered with UNMEB.
