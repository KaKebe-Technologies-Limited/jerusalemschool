About Us

Jerusalem Institute of Health Sciences Lira is a private multi-disciplinary health training institute which was started in 2010. It is registered by the Government of Uganda through the Ministry of Education and Sports under registration number; MOESTS/BTVET/147 for Jerusalem Institute of Health Science & Technology and ME/VOC/153 for Jerusalem School of Nursing & Midwifery.

Our Vision

To be a reputable and Innovative Institute of Excellence, training God fearing and skilled workers.

Our Mission

To produce quality, competent and skilled health workers with a positive attitude towards Health Care delivery and practice in communities through hands on training, developing and mentoring.

Core Values

- Godliness
- Compassion
- Team work
- Diversity
- Integrity & Professionalism
- Excellence & Accountability

Objectives

  • To establish a complete college of health sciences offering all health courses at all levels from certificate to degree.
  • To guide, council and mentor students to become responsible health professionals.
  • To participate in community activities.
  • To collaborate with relevant partners in education and health practice.
  • To train and assess students in both classrooms and clinical/field placements by implementing their regular curricula.
